Light-sheet Laser Speckle Imaging

Light-sheet laser speckle imaging is a labelfree flow mapping technique; Take advantage of the optical sectioning capability; Tomographic imaging with high spatiotemporal resolution; Excellent solution to investigating fluid dynamics in sophisticated 3D networks; Have numerous potential clinical applications and play an increasingly important role in translational medicine.

Technology Features

  1. Oblique plane illumination to improve SNR;
  2. Flexible light sheet thickness and length;
  3. Time-frequency analysis method retrieving velocity information;
  4. Independent cross validation/calibration.

Benefits

  1. Adequate photon budget;
  2. Fast depth scanning for 3D reconstruction;
  3. Simplify the sample preparation process;
  4. Label-free imaging platform.

TD-DOT device





Monitoring Hemodynamics

  1. TD-DOT irradiates live tissues by ultrashort pulsed near-infrared beams and detects the TPSF signals with picosecond resolution, which can be converted to media optical properties for quantifying chromophore concentrations with depth sensitivity.
  2. TD-DOT is an economical, portable, non-invasive instrument for continuous monitoring of blood dynamics, tissue oxygenation and oxidative metabolism in research and clinical applications.





Studying Brain Function

TD-DOT is exploited to measure cerebral hemodynamic responses to functional stimulation and provide the spatiotemporal dynamics of physiological signals for investigating pathological features of psychiatric disorders and neurodegenerative diseases.





Imaging Breast Tumor

Non-invasive, fast, high-resolution, functional optical imaging for future breast cancer screening, optical biopsy, and cancer treatment planning and monitoring.
